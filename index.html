Open Collections

Turn everyday folders into collections people can browse and share.

Open Collections helps teams organize files, publish clear collections, and reuse the same collection across websites and apps.

You start with a folder and end with a shareable, web-native collection. No lock-in, no custom platform migration, and no need to restructure your whole workflow before you begin.

Get started Explore tools Read Docs

How it works

1. Organize files into a collection folder

Keep related files together so content, media, and context stay in one place.

2. Manage and publish with practical tools

Use Collection Manager to add structure and details, then publish to your preferred hosting.

3. Browse and share anywhere

Open collections in Collection Browser, link them on your site, or embed them where people already work.

Why teams use it

Simple for non-technical teams

A familiar folder-and-files model makes it easy to start, maintain, and hand over work.

Open for technical teams

Developers can integrate, automate, and extend workflows without forcing everyone onto one closed system.

Web-native from day one

Collections are designed to be published and shared on the web, with control staying with the content owner.
