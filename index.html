Open Collections

Webcollections are like websites for cultural collections.

The Open Collections Protocol makes it simple to publish and share cultural collections on the web in an open format built on plain web standards. No database required. Host them anywhere, link them across the web, and keep them reusable over time.

Start from the files you already have

Many collections already exist as folders of images, documents, scans, or media. Open Collections helps you turn those files into a structured collection without forcing you into a complicated system from the start.

One collection can live in one folder, making it easier to understand, move, and maintain over time.

Designed for archives, museums, and design libraries

Whether you are working with archival material, object photography, exhibition assets, material libraries, or product collections, Open Collections helps bring files and metadata together in a way that stays understandable for both teams and visitors.

Organize collections for real use

Use the Collection Manager to prepare and structure a collection. Use the Collection Browser to explore it in a clear visual interface. This helps teams manage collection content internally and makes it easier to share collections online.

Gather

Bring together folders of images, documents, scans, and media from your existing collection work.

Organize

Use Collection Manager to structure files and details so teams can maintain collections with confidence.

Browse and share

Open the result in Collection Browser so colleagues and audiences can explore and use the collection.

Simple for collection teams, flexible for technical support

Open Collections is designed to be approachable for curators, archivists, librarians, collection managers, and design teams, while still giving developers and technical colleagues a clear foundation for hosting, integration, and extension.

That means teams can begin with a familiar workflow and grow into more advanced setups when needed.

Keep collections portable and understandable

Collections should remain usable over time. Open Collections is built around clear structures that help content stay portable, maintainable, and easier to reuse across platforms, tools, and teams.

Technical details about protocol, hosting options, and implementation are available in the Docs.
