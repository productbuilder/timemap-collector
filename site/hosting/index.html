Hosting

Choose a hosting setup that matches your team. The main goal is simple: keep your collection files available at stable web addresses.

Get started Hosting details in Docs

Common hosting choices

Git-based storage

A good fit for teams that want version history and review-based updates.

S3-compatible storage

A good fit for larger media libraries and high-volume delivery.

Mixed setup

Keep collection files in one place and larger media files in another if needed.

What to check before sharing

  • Your collection URL opens reliably.
  • Item and media links are stable.
  • Teammates can access the files they need.
