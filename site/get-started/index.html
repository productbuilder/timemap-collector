Get started with your own files

Use this page for a calm, practical first run. You can go from a folder on your computer to a shareable collection in a few steps.

1. Prepare your files

  • Create a folder for the files you want in one collection.
  • Use clear file names and remove anything you do not want to share.
  • Keep related files together so browsing feels natural.

2. Open Collection Manager

Open Collection Manager in your browser and start a new collection from your folder.

Open Collection Manager

3. Choose your folder

Point the tool to your collection folder. It reads your files so you can review everything in one place.

4. Add titles and details

Add a collection title, short description, and item details that help people understand what they are viewing.

5. Save and share

Save your collection, then share the URL with your team or audience. You can keep improving it over time.
