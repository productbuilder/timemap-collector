Registry and indexer

These services support multi-collection discovery workflows.

Registry role

  • Track known collection endpoints.
  • Record validation/indexing status.
  • Support federation between registries.

Indexer role

  • Fetch collection data from known endpoints.
  • Create searchable indexes for browser/API use.
  • Refresh data on schedule or events.
