Collection Registry and Collection Indexer

These roles are related but distinct. Registry tracks what is known; Indexer processes what is known into discovery and search outputs.

Collection Registry

  • register publishers and collection endpoints
  • track validation and indexing state
  • federate or link with other registries
  • preserve provenance for imported records

Collection Indexer

  • fetch manifests and item records from known URLs
  • create searchable and browsable derived indexes
  • refresh on schedule or event triggers
  • feed browser and aggregator experiences
